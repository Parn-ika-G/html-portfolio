Best thriller movies that you should definitely add to your movie list for this weekend!

Top 3 amazing movies to watch

1. World War Z

If you are a zombie-lover, then this movie will definitely scare the hell out of you! The movie revolves around an ex-UN employee, who goes beyond odds to protect his family from flesh eating zombies, simultaneously solving the mystery behind the virus outbreak.

2. Open Your Eyes

A 1997 Spanish sci-fi thriller is a story about a young man exploring his life, who suffers an accident and undergoes a face surgery which causes a series of ups and down. The storyline is truly mindblowing and might definitely get you lost in your dreams.

3. Parasite

The movie showcasts light upon the darker and real side of society's poor group and the ways they struggle to get acquainted into the mainflow of the society. It narrates about the problems faced by the Kim family and how they try to fulfil their very basic needs by depending on a rich family like a parasite!
